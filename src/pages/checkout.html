Embed Circularity
  1. Recycling : Full account

    Rather than sending all waste to the landfill, divert accepted materials for recycling.

  2. Mobile orders : Opt out of cutlery

    Provide an option in your mobile ordering system prompting consumers with the choice to decline cutlery.

  3. Waste bins : Signage

    Label waste bins based on destination (landfill, recycle, compost), to ensure that consumers are properly sorting their waste.
