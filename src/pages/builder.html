Embed Circularity

Reduce Single-Use Plastics.

When we use the term circular, we refer to our ability to keep materials in the economy in circulation and out of the environment. These solutions are focused on redesigning operations with circularity in mind through reducing the consumption of single-use plastics and other disposables, and expanding opportunities to reuse, recycle, and compost. An efficient circular system generates little to no waste.
